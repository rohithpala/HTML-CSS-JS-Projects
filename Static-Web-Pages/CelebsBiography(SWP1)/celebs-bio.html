Hello, My Name is Nani

Ghanta Naveen Babu (born 24 February 1984), known professionally as Nani, is an Indian film actor, producer, and television presenter primarily known for his work in Telugu cinema. Nani made his film debut with the 2008 romantic comedy Ashta Chamma and went on to star in many commercially successful films.
